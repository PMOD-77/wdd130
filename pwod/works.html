Place Holder Series

Place Holder: In The Echoes of Hollow Pines, a small, isolated town hides a chilling secret—one that has kept its residents trapped in an endless cycle of fear and silence. When investigative journalist Lila Mercer arrives in Hollow Pines to report on a string of mysterious disappearances, she quickly realizes that the town's past is woven with eerie folklore and sinister rituals. The deeper she digs, the more she is haunted by whispers in the woods, cryptic warnings from frightened locals, and a presence that seems to be watching her every move. As the boundary between reality and nightmare blurs, Lila must unravel the town’s dark history before she becomes its next victim. But in Hollow Pines, some secrets are never meant to be unearthed.

Place Holder: In The Shadows of Hollow Pines, investigative journalist Lila Mercer thought she had escaped the horrors of the cursed town—but Hollow Pines was never finished with her. A year after her narrow escape, she begins experiencing terrifying visions, cryptic nightmares, and an unshakable feeling that something is watching her. When a desperate call for help pulls her back to the isolated town, she discovers that the disappearances have started again, and the eerie whispers in the woods have only grown stronger. As she delves deeper into the town’s dark past, Lila uncovers the existence of an ancient force buried beneath Hollow Pines—something far more sinister than she ever imagined. This time, escaping won’t be enough. If Lila wants to survive, she must face the darkness head-on… before it claims her soul forever.

Place Holder: In The Reckoning of Hollow Pines, the cursed town’s dark secrets are finally unraveling, and Lila Mercer knows there’s only one way to end its nightmare once and for all. The ancient force that has haunted Hollow Pines for centuries is awakening, growing stronger with every soul it consumes. The whispers in the woods have become deafening, and the town itself seems to be shifting—twisting into something unrecognizable. As Lila pieces together the final clues hidden in the town’s bloodstained history, she realizes that she is the key to breaking the cycle. But stopping the darkness means making an unthinkable sacrifice. With time running out and the boundary between reality and nightmare crumbling, Lila must confront the entity that has been waiting for her all along. In the end, Hollow Pines will either be freed—or she will be lost to it forever.

Place Holder: In The Clockmaker’s Paradox, renowned horologist Elias Vance discovers an antique pocket watch with an impossible design—one that seems to tick backward, bending time itself. When he repairs the mysterious device, he unwittingly unlocks a temporal rift that pulls him into a labyrinth of alternate realities, each more distorted than the last. Hunted by enigmatic figures who claim the watch is the key to a lost civilization, Elias must navigate shifting timelines and cryptic riddles to uncover the truth. But as past and future collide, he faces an impossible choice: restore the balance of time or risk being erased from existence forever.

Place Holder: In The Clockmaker’s Gambit, Elias Vance thought he had escaped the chaos of time’s unraveling—but time hasn’t finished with him yet. When a strange anomaly begins distorting reality, pulling objects and people from lost eras into the present, Elias is forced to once again seek answers within the mysterious pocket watch that started it all. But this time, he’s not the only one searching. A secret society known as the Order of the Pendulum believes the watch holds the key to controlling time itself, and they will stop at nothing to claim it. As Elias is thrust into a dangerous game of cat and mouse across shifting timelines, he discovers an even more terrifying truth—time isn’t breaking; it’s being rewritten. And unless he stops the Order, history itself may be erased forever.

Place Holder: In The Clockmaker’s End, time itself is collapsing, and Elias Vance is the only one who can stop it. The pocket watch—once a key to unraveling the mysteries of time—has become an instrument of destruction, its power now unstable and threatening to erase entire timelines. The Order of the Pendulum has harnessed its energy, seeking to reshape history in their own image, and the past, present, and future are beginning to fracture beyond repair. With reality breaking apart around him, Elias must embark on one final journey through the labyrinth of time, uncovering the watch’s true origins and the ancient force that has been manipulating history all along. But saving time will require the ultimate sacrifice, and Elias must decide: will he destroy the watch and restore order, or wield its power one last time—at the risk of being lost to eternity? In this final chapter, the clock strikes its last hour, and time’s reckoning begins.

Place Holder: The Hollow Crown, For centuries, the kingdom of Eldrin has been ruled by the divine right of the Hollow Crown, a legendary artifact said to choose its rightful ruler. But when the aging king dies without an heir, the crown remains silent, throwing the realm into chaos. In The Hollow Crown, orphaned thief Kael discovers he has an unnatural connection to the crown—one that could make him king or mark him for death. Hunted by noble factions, assassins, and a rising rebellion, Kael must uncover the crown’s true purpose before Eldrin falls into ruin.

Place Holder: The Shattered Throne, Kael’s claim to the Hollow Crown is challenged by an exiled princess with a bloodright to the throne. As the kingdom teeters on the brink of war, an ancient enemy stirs in the shadows, threatening to consume everything. With magic returning to the land and alliances breaking apart, Kael must decide whether to fight for a throne he never wanted—or destroy the power that binds it.

Place Holder: The Last King, where the Hollow Crown reveals its final secret: it was never meant to be worn, only broken. As dark forces rise and the kingdom fractures, Kael must face the truth of his destiny. With the fate of Eldrin at stake, he will either become the ruler history needs—or the last king to ever sit upon the throne.
